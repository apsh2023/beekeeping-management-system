# Beekeeping Management System

Welcome to our comprehensive business design project focusing on data modeling for beekeeping management.

## Project Overview

This project presents a detailed analysis and design for a modern beekeeping management system, addressing the needs of both individual beekeepers and commercial apiaries.

## Key Features

- **Hive Management**: Track and monitor individual hives
- **Honey Production**: Record and analyze honey yields
- **Health Monitoring**: Track bee colony health and treatments
- **Equipment Management**: Maintain inventory of beekeeping equipment
- **Financial Tracking**: Monitor costs and revenue streams
